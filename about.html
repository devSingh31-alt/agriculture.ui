The Problem

Agricultural Supply Chain Challenges

  • Price Opacity

    Farmers don't know true market prices and often get exploited by middlemen

  • Inefficient Logistics

    Poor route planning leads to delays, spoilage, and waste

  • Middlemen Dominance

    Multiple intermediaries take 30-40% of the final price

  • High Food Wastage

    30% of produce is wasted due to supply chain inefficiencies

Our Solution

FarmLink Platform Benefits

  • Transparent Pricing

    Real-time market data ensures farmers get fair, competitive prices

  • Smart Routing

    AI-powered logistics reduce transit time by 40% and minimize waste

  • Direct Connection

    Cut out middlemen and farmers keep 60% more of their earnings

  • Waste Reduction

    Better matching and tracking reduces waste by 70%
